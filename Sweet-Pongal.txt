Also known as Sarkarai Pongal in Tamil.
This is a South Indian Dish prepared on Festival Days.

Ingredients:
 - 1 cup Rice -- slightly dry roasted
 - 1/2 cup Moong Dal -- slightly dry roasted
 - 2 cups Jaggery or Brown Sugar
 - 1/2 cup Ghee
 - 1 table spoon Cashew nuts (roasted in Ghee)
 - 1/2 teaspoon ground Cardamom

Cooking Directions:
 1. Cook Rice and Dal together in a pressure cooker (3 cups water).
 
 2. In a large pan, heat up jaggery (sugar) in 1/2 cup water and bring to boil.
 
 3. Add cooked Rice and Dal into the pan. Mix thoroughly after adding the Ghee.
 
 4. Add roasted Cashew nuts and Cardamom for garnishing and taste.
